AKAN NAMES

The Akan are mostly located along the coastal and central regions of Ghana and the Ivory Coast, with some others in Diaspora also claim ancestry. Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
